# Sales Analysis Report for the Year 2020

![](https://github.com/Cuteod/Sales-Analysis-Report-for-the-Year-2020/blob/main/stats-concept-with-wood-blocks.jpg)

## Introduction

In businesses, analysing performances helps drive revenue and profit. This analysis contains a regional sales performance analysis conducted on 2020 sales data. It highlights revenue trends, top product categories, customer segmentation, and key insights across four regions—Eastern, Northern, Southern, and Western—using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to provide actionable insights and recommendations to improve sales strategy and operations.
 
The project aims to answer:
- How does sales performance vary across regions and months?  
- Which product categories drive the most revenue?  
- Who are the top customers and cities for sales?  
- What strategic recommendations can be made from observed patterns?

## Dataset Description  
The dataset includes details on orders, customers, salespersons, products, and transactions. Key variables:

- Order ID, Order Date, Shipped Date  
- Customer Name, Region, Salesperson  
- Product Name, Category, Unit Price, Quantity, Revenue  
- City, Shipping Fee, Payment Type

## Tools Used  
- **Microsoft Excel**: Data cleaning, analysis, and dashboard visualization.

### Regional Performance  
| Region     | Total Revenue | Peak Month           |
|------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Eastern    | $108,275.51   | June                 |
| Northern   | $141,680.34   | December             |
| Southern   | $93,858.33    | December             |

- The **Northern region** recorded the highest total revenue, with a strong holiday peak.  
- The **Eastern region** uniquely peaked in June, suggesting distinct seasonal behavior.  
- Other regions showed winter/holiday season dominance.

## Top Product Categories by Region

- **Eastern:** Jams & Preserves, Dried Fruit & Nuts, Condiments, Baked Goods & Mixes, Pasta & Grains  
- **Northern:** Beverages, Sauces, Candy, Dairy Products, Dried Fruit & Nuts, Canned Meat  
- **Southern:** Beverages, Canned Meat, Sauces, Oil, Soups, Dried Fruit & Nuts  
- **Western:** Beverages, Dairy Products, Sauces, Pasta, Fruits & Vegetables, Soups

## Top Cities & Customers

### Top Ship Cities

- **Southern:** Miami, Memphis  
- **Western:** Salt Lake City, Los Angeles, Denver, Las Vegas

### Top Customers by Region
- **Eastern:** Company D, Company J, Company Y  
- **Northern:** Company H, Company F, Company A, Company G  
- **Southern:** Company BB, Company Z, Company K  
- **Western:** Company I, Company C, Company CC, Company L, Company AA  

## Insights

- Most regions peak in **December**, driven by holiday demand.  
- **Eastern region’s June spike** suggests mid-year promotional opportunities.  
- Sales performance often concentrates among a few key customers, posing a customer concentration risk.

## Recommendations

1. **Strengthen Regional Marketing**: Tailor campaigns to seasonality (e.g., summer push for Eastern region).  
2. **Enhance Sales Training**: Provide targeted coaching for salespersons with inconsistent performance.  
3. **Optimize Inventory Planning**: Forecast demand before peak months to minimize stockouts.  
4. **Expand Customer Base**: Reduce dependence on a small number of top accounts.  
5. **Leverage High-Value Cities**: Focus promotions in major cities with high sales activity.

## Conclusion  
This analysis provides a comprehensive view of 2020 sales performance across regions. The insights can inform strategic decisions around inventory, marketing, and sales resource allocation to boost future performance.
